Joevita's Travel Bucket List

This page is all about my travel bucket list. I've been wanting to visit these countries someday.

For the About button : I used show() and hide(). I also used toggle() for the more explanation button. I also provide a button to make the about section become transparant with toggle() so it won't disturb you when you want to click the other button.

For the America button : I used slideDown() and slideUp(). Inside the button, there are Los Angeles and New York button where I used slideToggle().

For the Australia button : I used fadeIn() and fadeOut(). Inside the button, there are Sydney and Melbourne button where I used fadeToggle().

For the Canada button : I combine slide and fade. First I used slideDown() and slideUp() and then inside the button, there are Vancouver and Quebec button where I used fadeToggle().



For the Europe button : I combine fade and slide again. First I used fadeIn() and fadeOut() and then inside the button, there is Prague button where I used slideToggle().
